Enrollment information

Documents required for registration

When applying, students must submit the following documents:

  • Admission (free from school)
  • Original certificates from VI to IX grade
  • Birth certificate
  • Immunization confirmation
  • Diplomas (if any), from I, II or III place won in international and national competitions

Competition Deadlines

The first application and enrollment of students in the June enrollment period is done June 14-17, 2022, and enrollment results and vacancies will be announced on June 18, 2022.
The second application from the June enrollment period is done June 23 to 25, 2022, and enrollment results and vacancies will be announced on July 1, 2022.
The third registration and enrollment of students will be done on June 30, 2022, and enrollment results and vacancies will be announced on July 1, 2022.
Students who did not fill in the required deadlines in the two enrollment deadlines Student places for enrollment with this competition continue with enrollment of students up to 31 August 2022 year and enrollment of students will be done on June 30, 2022 , and enrollment results and vacancies will be announced on July 1, 2022.
