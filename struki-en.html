Proffesions


Electrotechnical

This is one of the most attractive professions in our school, in which there are five directions:
computer technology and automation, electronics and telecommunications,
power engineer, electrical installer and installer and electrical mechanic.

Mechanical

With the development of industry, technology and science, the number of enrolled students is increasing and
the diversity of classes of different profiles in the mechanical profession.
Electrician, electrician and installer and electrician contribute to that.

Traffic

The development of the traffic profession in the school begins with the establishment of the driving school.
It provides training for students who will gain knowledge in this profession, and at the same time will
can obtain a permit.
