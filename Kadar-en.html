School staff

  • School principal - Spirko Zlatkov

    The principal is a pedagogical worker in the school, who plans, programs, organizes and monitors the realization of the educational work in the school. He participates and manages the management and professional team in the school. Collaborates and represents the school locally and on a regional, national and international level. The director performs other activities determined by the Statute and the Law, in cooperation with the Local government after the decentralization of education and the Ministry of Education and Science.
