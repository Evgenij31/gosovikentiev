Our mission

"Gosho Vikentiev" Secondary School - Kočani is a school with more than 50 years of existence, which is always open for new ideas that encourage individuality and creativity, cooperation and agreement, development of higher intellectual abilities and education to be able to respond to animals challenges. Ours the school is a democratic multicultural community, in which the student is always at the center the attention. Our core mission is to enable each student to express and develop their own abilities and personality traits by tracking mandatory, optional, additional, and supplementary teaching.




Our vision

We strive for a school that is in step with all social changes, where the reforms in continuity lasts, and students have the opportunity to satisfy their various educational needs needs. Through mutual respect and cooperation, we encourage students to develop skills, knowledge and attitudes, necessary for self-actualization, as responsible and educated citizens. We love school which has an active role in society in accordance with the interests of society. With the planned activities, programs and projects, we strive for quality results to achieve the goals directed towards the student and his development, where great attention is paid to learning and teaching, the responsibility and independence of both the student and the teacher. About our school

"Gosho Vikentiev" Secondary School was founded by the Decision of the People's Board of Kočani on December 5, 1959 how industrial school with practical training. Until the formation of the school level, the classes were worked in the basement room of the school building. They trained metal turners, metal cutters, locksmiths and locksmiths. In the school year 1962/63, the school started its own building in the current Primary School school "Malina Popivanova".



Seventies

In the 1970s, the first electrical engineering profession was opened, the electrical engineer-power engineer major. Then the school grows into the first Electromechanical school center in the eastern part of Macedonia, covering students from Gevgelija to Delchevo. The number of classes is more than fifteen.



Eighties

In the eighties of the last century, the school increased by more than thirty classes. The machine the profession is rounded off with all the necessary profiles, the electrician profile has been added to the electrical technician and fitter, the graphic profession was introduced, as well as the profile of drivers in the traffic profession. In parallel with the growth of qualified teaching staff, the Assembly of Municipality of Kočani. The new school building, the current one, was put into use on September 1, 1985. In accordance with the needs of industrial facilities in the nineties of the last century, "Rouen", "Paper", "Atom", "Pechatnitsa", etc., profiles are opening: chemical-technological profession, masons-façades, new profiles on the graphic profession.



Our school today

Today, the school has 1278 students in 38 classes, in three professions (traffic, mechanical and electrical engineering) and 11 profiles. Teaching is carried out by 67 professors organized in 7 professional assets. In addition to receiving a school diploma, every student is given the opportunity to: he also obtained a driver's license. From its establishment until today, the school has been growing both in terms of the number of students, teachers, and professionals profiles, modern teaching technology, space and modern curricula. Especially with the entry into the new millennium, there will be further modernization of the teaching by supplementing it and redirection to new profiles from three-year and four-year education in electrical, mechanical and the traffic profession. The last open profiles in the four-year education are: "technician for computer management" (mechanical profession), "electrical technician for computer technology and automation" (electrical profession) and "transportation and forwarding technician" (traffic profession). With the current rounded capacity, we count 42 classes. The school has a sports hall with modern infrastructure, sports fields, paved driving range and ecologically landscaped school yard.



Youth organization "Avangarda"

In parallel with formal education, we monitor the needs of young people for their factoring in society. They we carry out activities through the youth organization "Avangarda" and the Office for Career Development. The three key features of the school's functioning are: the personnel structure, the modern classroom teaching and the development component. It gives it the image of a desirable school for learning and working in the region. The school breasts partnership relations with other schools from the country and abroad, with companies and other social partners in the country. The multidisciplinary development in the field of automation, robotics and innovation, as well as remarkable good luck which our students achieve in domestic and international competitions, give us hope that SOU "Gosho Vikentiev" still has something to show in the coming period.



Specialized rooms

The school has 20 specialized classrooms - cabinets for high-quality performance of the theoretical teaching, 5 workshops for narrower practical teaching, 5 specialized cabinets for practical teaching and car service. We have a European standard of useful area per student of 9.72 m2. The corridors, stairs, sanitary units and other auxiliary rooms in the school building. The eight offices are enough for the manager staff, the pedagogical service and the administration. There is also enough warehouse and storage space at the school. Project work in continuity is a feature of modern teaching in specialized classrooms, offices, the applicable practice in own workshops and teamwork in the research and advancement of the teaching process and program.
