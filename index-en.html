About us

"Gosho Vikentiev" Secondary School - Kočani is a school with more than 50 years of existence, which is always open for new ideas that encourage individuality and creativity, cooperation and agreement, development of higher intellectual abilities and education that will be able to respond to life's challenges. Our school is a democratic multicultural community, in which the student is always at the center the attention.

Vision

of the school

We strive for a school that is in step with all social changes, where the reforms in continuity last, and students have the opportunity to meet their various educational needs.
