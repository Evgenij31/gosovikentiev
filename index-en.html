MHS „GOSHO VIKENTIEV” - Kocani

The high school "Goso Vikentiev" was established by a decision of the People's Board of Kocani on December 5, 1959 year as an industrial school with practical training. Until the formation of the level of school, the classes were worked on in the basement of the building the school. Metalworkers, metalworkers, locksmiths and locksmiths were trained. In the school year 1962/63 the school started its own building in the current primary school "Malina Popivanova".
